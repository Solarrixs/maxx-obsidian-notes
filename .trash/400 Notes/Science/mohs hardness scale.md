The Mohs Hardness Scale is a rough measure of a mineral’s hardness, determined by the mineral’s resistance to abrasion by a substance of a known hardness.

In order from 1-10:
1. talc
2. gypsum
3. calcite
4. fluorite
5. apatite
6. orthoclase
7. [[quartz]]
8. topaz
9. corundum
10. diamond
