## Proposal
Either through [[congress]] or by states calling for a convention with a 2/3 majority to propose an [[amendment]].
- Every amendment thus proposed has been through the Congress.

## Ratification
Either state legislatures or ratifying conventions at states with a 3/4 majority to ratify an amendment.
- Only the 21st [[amendment]] was done through state ratifying conventions.
